Bitcoin

I owe a lot to Bitcoin. It has allowed me to take care of myself and do what I love in life. After my health and my family, Bitcoin is the most important thing in the world to me. I play for team Bitcoin and I’m supported by Bitcoiners. I live a low time preference life in alignment with the values of Bitcoin: Truth, Freedom & Personal responsibility. I run a node, hold my own keys and I’m working towards running a nostr relay on my own home server. My life is dedicated to upgrading the world by increasing health & financial literacy within the pickleball community. Once a month I update my sponsor stats: # of zaps/donations, total # of sats received.

Pickleball

I love playing pickleball and spend much of my time at a local court training, coaching newbies, and playing. I’m a v4v movement coach and training partner. People pay me sats to help them move better and to play with them as a doubles partner in tournaments. I play for Bitcoin and mention Bitcoin to everyone I play against. I also offer to send a few sats so they can experience magic internet money first hand. I wear Bitcoin branded clothing to play and I’m currently setting up a magic webstore (a simple, private ecommerce store built on Bitcoin) to sell high quality Bitcoin swag which helps support my pro career and my family. My first product is a hat. I made 12 of them and they are available for purchase. Each hat is signed by me, numbered and time stamped with Moscow time. DM me on NOSTR if you want one.
